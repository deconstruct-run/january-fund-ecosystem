How Value Flows

Understanding the investor journey from onboarding through value realization.

1

Investor Verification

Prospective investors complete identity verification, accreditation checks, and regulatory compliance screening through integrated onboarding systems.

2

Token Subscription

Verified investors subscribe to Master Tokens under applicable securities exemptions. Tokens are minted and delivered to the investor's verified wallet address.

3

Capital Deployment

Subscription capital flows into the master vehicle and is allocated across operating sectors and treasury operations according to strategic priorities.

4

Value Generation

Sectors generate operating income. Treasury generates yield. Profits are reinvested to compound growth. All value accrues to the master vehicle.

5

NAV Appreciation

As master vehicle value increases, token NAV rises proportionally. Investors can track their holdings' value on-chain with transparent, verifiable accounting.
